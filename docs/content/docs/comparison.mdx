## When Better Auth Ruby Fits

Choose Better Auth Ruby when you want full ownership of the auth system without
writing every protocol, provider, route, and schema detail yourself. It is a good
fit for teams that prefer explicit Ruby code, database-level visibility,
framework flexibility, and infrastructure they can operate directly.

It is not trying to be a managed identity platform. You bring your UI, email
provider, deployment, observability, and operational standards. In return, you
keep the auth layer understandable, portable, and under your control.
